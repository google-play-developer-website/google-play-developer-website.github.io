MemoryCard

Welcome to our Memory Card app, a tool specifically designed to enhance your memory and boost your learning efficiency. All data are stored locally to protect your privacy and provide a more secure experience. You can export all your cards to a file and import them back into the app. Our app offers a simple yet effective method to help you remember crucial information.

Pomodoro Focus

Welcome to our Pomodoro Timer app, an application designed to enhance your work efficiency and focus. Our app is based on the renowned Pomodoro Technique, dividing your work time into 25-minute work intervals, with a 5-minute or 15-minute break between each interval. This method can help you manage time better, boost work efficiency, reduce distractions, and enhance focus.

DailyProtein

Calculate your daily protein needs based on your weight with our DailyProtein app. Your health is our priority! Our app is designed to conveniently calculate your daily protein intake based on your body weight. Our calculation is tailored to adults, ensuring that the unique dietary needs are fulfilled. Leveraging research from the World Health Organization (WHO) CINDI pyramid and insights from Mayo Clinic Health System, our protein calculations align with top global health standards. But our app doesn’t stop there! To ensure you reach your daily protein target, we guide you on the type and quantity of food you should consume. Healthy eating is now simplified and made accessible to everyone. Embrace a healthier lifestyle, start this journey with us today!
